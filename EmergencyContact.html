Emergency Contacts

Reach out to the appropriate emergency service immediately if needed.

Emergency Hotlines: 999 (Police/Ambulance) | 994 (Fire)

Important Reminders:

  • • In case of life-threatening emergency, call 999 immediately
  • • For campus emergencies after hours, contact Campus Security
  • • Keep these numbers saved in your phone
  • • Stay calm and provide clear information
  • • Know your exact location
